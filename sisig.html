SISIG recipe

Description

a Filipino dish made from pork jowl and ears (maskara), pork belly, and chicken liver, which is usually seasoned with calamansi, onions, and chili peppers.

It originates from the Pampanga region in Luzon.

Ingredients

  • Pork
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Egg
  • Garlic
  • Red Onion
  • Rice
  • Soy sauce
  • Mayonnaise
  • Spring Onion

Steps

  1. Fry egg in vegetable oil until edges are crispy. Set aside.
  2. Remove excess oil for the pan leaving 1 tbsp. Sauté garlic until light golden brown. Strain and set aside for topping
  3. Sauté pork in the same pan for 2 minutes. Stir in red onion, remove from the pan and set aside.
  4. Add rice in the same pan. Gently fluff for 2 minutes. Transfer on a serving plate and drizzle MAGGI® Savor Chilimansi.
